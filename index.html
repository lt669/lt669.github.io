MANDiLIN

MANDiLIN is an interactive mandolin application for the iPad designed to be easy to use for professional and amateur musicians everywhere. With the use of accurate physical models MANDiLIN lets you simulate the sound of a large range of different styles of mandolin. MANiLIN can also be used as an aid for beginner mandolin players with the use of the on screen notes preview! Built in instructions makes the app easy to from the start, with intuitive controls and interaction.
